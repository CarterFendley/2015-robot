1st Autonomus:
Stack 3 yellow totes, put them in autozone
	Sesnsors required:
		Gyro for positioning
		Accelerometer
		Camera at Tote level
		Infared at tote level
		PID and some sensor to zero the forklift
	Steps
	Zero the forklift
	Start behind white bump, going to gap between auto and starting zone
	go sideways until image processing says we are perpendicular to a tote
	go forward until ultrasonic says we are in pickup range, record how far we went
	pick up tote
	go backwards however far we went forwards
	go sideways until we reach a second tote and repeat until we hold 3
	go backwards into the auto zone and stack the totes
